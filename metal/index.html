Commissioned by an up-and-coming pro wrestler, this theme was requested to feature a camera shutter and an awesome hair metal scream.

Commissioned

Commissioned by a new content creator for their YouTube, Twitch, and podcast intro. The requirements were to include 8-bit and 16-bit chiptune.

Character Theme - Charlotte Biose

Composed with a charismatic thief from an RPG or stealth game in mind. It is designed to loop indefinitely. Not heavy metal but it's nice to have variety.
